PSVAR exemption assessment service

Accessibility regulations

Check whether your home-to-school fleet may qualify for a PSVAR exemption

Use this service to provide operator, fleet and exemption details for a home-to-school PSVAR exemption assessment.

The assistant will guide you through the questions in a form-like way and explain the outcome in plain English.

Before you start

  • Have your company and operator licence details ready.
  • Have the authorised contact’s telephone number, email and postal address ready.
  • Know your total fleet size and how many vehicles are fully, partially and non-compliant.
  • Prepare the VIN for each vehicle in scope.
  • If you already hold an exemption certificate, have the certificate reference and dates available.
Important: This version of the service is for home-to-school services only. Rail replacement services are not assessed in this workflow.

What the assistant will help with

Collect details

Guides you through company, contact, fleet and certificate information.

Validate entries

Checks VIN formatting and helps identify missing or inconsistent information.

Explain the outcome

Provides a clear assessment result, rationale and next actions.
