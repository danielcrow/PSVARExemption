About PSVAR Exemptions

The Public Service Vehicle Accessibility Regulations (PSVAR) require buses and coaches used for local or scheduled services to meet accessibility standards. However, exemptions may be available for certain home-to-school transport services.

Who can apply?

You may be eligible for a PSVAR exemption if you operate:

  • Closed-door home-to-school transport services
  • Services with paying customers
  • A fleet that meets specific compliance requirements based on your fleet size band

What you'll need:

  • Company and operator licence details
  • Contact information for correspondence
  • Fleet composition details (total vehicles, compliance status)
  • Vehicle Identification Numbers (VINs) for all vehicles
  • Information about operational commitments

Important: Make sure you have read the compliance requirements for your fleet size band before starting your application.

Start Your Application

Use the chat assistant below to complete your PSVAR exemption application. The assistant will guide you through the process step by step.

What happens next?

After you complete your application:

  1. Your eligibility will be assessed based on the information provided
  2. You'll receive an immediate decision on your application
  3. If eligible, you'll receive instructions on next steps
  4. If further investigation is required, a DVSA officer will contact you
